Tater tot Casserole

Fantastic One Pan Recipe!

Easy, simple, one pan, filling... What more do you need?

Ground beef mixed with assorted veggies and tater tots ontop

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 small onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • salt and pepper
  • 32 ounces frozen tater tots
  • Two 10.75 ounce cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a 9x13-inch pan with nonstick spray.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the beef and onion over medium heat until the meat is no longer pink; remove and discard the fat. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Transfer the meat mixture to the prepared baking dish. Top with the Tater Tots. In a medium bowl, whisk together the soup and milk. Pour over the potatoes. Sprinkle with the cheese. Bake, uncovered, for 40 minutes.
